# TTL Insurance SEO Content Cluster
### 5-Article Plan | Zero Ad Spend | 45–90 Day Organic Ramp

---

## Strategic Logic

Each article targets a **post-total-loss search intent** that naturally leads to an
insurance quote request. All articles end with the same CTA routing to the TTL chatbot
with insurance opt-in pre-activated.

The content cluster strategy works because Google's Helpful Content algorithm rewards
**topical depth** — owning 5+ related articles on the same topic signals expertise
and boosts all articles in the cluster simultaneously.

---

## Article 1: Post-accident confusion (Widest funnel)

**Target Keyword:** `"my car was totaled what happens to my insurance"`  
**Search Volume:** ~4,400/mo | **Keyword Difficulty:** Low  
**Intent:** Confused, overwhelmed, immediately post-accident  
**Word Count:** 1,800 words

### Outline:
1. What "total loss" means for your insurance policy (it usually lapses immediately)
2. The 7-day gap window — why you're uninsured right now in Texas
3. What happens to your current policy (cancelled vs. suspended)
4. What your settlement check does and doesn't cover
5. The 3 things you need to do in the next 48 hours (includes insurance shopping)

**CTA:** "Use our free Texas Total Loss Settlement Checker → then get quotes from local agents in 60 seconds."

## Article 2: Urgency / Deadline-Driven

**CTA:** "Talk to our team about your settlement number → then get matched with Texas insurers who reward not-at-fault drivers."

## Article 5: Tool-based lead magnet (Shareability + High conversion)
