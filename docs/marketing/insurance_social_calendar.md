## Strategy

Every piece of content serves one of three jobs:
1. **Educate** — Teach the audience something they didn't know (builds trust, gets shared)
2. **Engage** — Interactive formats (quizzes, polls, "which one are you?")
3. **Convert** — Direct CTA to the insurance quiz or chatbot opt-in

The ratio for organic success: **60% Educate / 25% Engage / 15% Convert**
